50-50 Rule


Schools of Chefland use the 50 − 50 50−50 rule to grade their students. As per the rule , students are awarded : Z grade, if their attendance is strictly less than 50 % 50% . F grade, if they score strictly less than 50 % 50% marks, given that their attendance is greater than or equal to 50 % 50% . A grade, otherwise. You are given two integers, X and Y, denoting the percentage of attendance and percentage of marks obtained by Alice. What grade will Alice get?

Sample Input:

4
49 100
49 49
50 49
50 50

Sample Output:

Z
Z
F
A
