Dice Game 3


Chef is playing a game consisting of N rounds. In each round he rolls a regular six faced dice. He starts with an initial score of 0 0. Let the number rolled by him in a particular round be X. If this is not the first round and the previous number rolled was 1 1, Chef will add 2 ⋅ 2⋅X to his score, otherwise he will add X to his score. For example, if the game has 6 6 rounds and the rolls are [ 2 , 1 , 6 , 1 , 1 , 4 ] [2,1,6,1,1,4] in the given order, his score will be 2 + 1 + ( 2 ⋅ 6 ) + 1 + ( 2 ⋅ 1 ) + ( 2 ⋅ 4 ) = 26 2+1+(2⋅6)+1+(2⋅1)+(2⋅4)=26. Find the maximum possible score he can obtain after N rounds.

Sample Input:

2
2
4

Sample Output:

13
26
